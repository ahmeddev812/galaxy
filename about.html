About Us

The Group

Arun Shuttle Industries was founded as privately owned company in 1982, as a manufacturer of non original textile spinning and weaving machinery spares. We are a progressive and professional ISO 9001 : 2008 certified company located Ahmedabad (India).

Since our inception in 1982, we have gradually grown from product to product and now we manufacture and have supplied to more than 1500 different customers. Today the journey we started with 1 manufacturing plant and 10 people has grown to 4 manufacturing plant with more then 300 people.

Inhouse Tool Room

We have one of the most well equipped tool design & tool manufacturing facilities among all textile machinery and spares manufacturer. Precision state-of-art CNC machines for milling, spark erosion, wire cut operations, fine surface grinding are in operation.

We also have separate group of engineers specialized in tool design capability for developing of press toll using contemporary software tools.Mass production of most textile machinery spares require precision press tools to produce press metal parts. We manufacture tools & dies within our own tool rooms.

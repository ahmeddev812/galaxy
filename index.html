Welcome to ARUN SHUTTLE INDUSTRIES

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as one of the foremost Manufacturer / Exporter of Textile Spinning Spares and Machines for Spinning / Weaving / Processing / Plastic Woven Sacks and Roll Shop. The company was set up in the early 80s, with an initial emphasis on manufacturing Shuttle and Pirns. Later on, we developed various machines and accessories for Spinning, Weaving, Processing and Woven Sack Industry and have created a niche market for our products in India and Overseas based on Quality, Consistency, Precision and Dimensional Accuracy of our manufacturing.

Global Footprint

We have been serving 1500+ Customers across 36+ countries Worldwide since our inception in 1982. An extreme focus on client satisfaction has helped us grow.
